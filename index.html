Academic Pressure

A healthier measure of progress

In many education systems, students are judged mainly by grades. This may make assessment efficient, but it can also make teenagers anxious. When young people spend most of the week preparing for tests, sleep and hobbies are often treated as unimportant. A healthier system should value effort while protecting time for rest, exercise and social life.

许多教育体系主要以成绩评价学生。这样做或许便于评估，却也可能令青少年焦虑。更健康的制度应重视努力，同时保障休息、运动与社交时间。

  2. What should a healthier system protect?
