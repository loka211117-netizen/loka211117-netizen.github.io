本周模板：Teenage Pressure

Hong Kong has an ossified education system that focuses too much on academic results. Schools and parents force students to perform well. As a result, teenagers have little time to sleep or play. Like me, I often have sleepless nights because I have to go to school five or even six days a week.

香港教育制度比较僵化，太重视成绩。学校和家长都逼学生考好，所以青少年没时间睡觉或玩。像我一样，我经常睡不好，因为一周要上五六天课。

Topic: Academic Pressure

In many education systems, students are judged mainly by grades. This makes schools efficient, but it can also make teenagers anxious. When a young person spends most of the week preparing for tests, sleep and hobbies are often treated as unimportant. A healthier system should still value effort, but it should also protect time for rest, exercise and social life.

很多教育体系主要用成绩评价学生。这让学校管理更有效率，但也会让青少年焦虑。当一个年轻人一周大部分时间都在备考，睡眠和兴趣常被视为不重要。更健康的制度仍然应该重视努力，但也要保护休息、运动和社交时间。

Q1. What problem can grade-focused education create?

Q2. Find one phrase meaning “not important”.

Q3. What should a healthier system protect?

A1: It can make teenagers anxious.

A2: Treated as unimportant.

A3: Time for rest, exercise and social life.
